Our Story

Born from Flame & Heritage

Founded in 2012 in the heart of Karachi, Ember & Spice was born from a passion for authentic flavors and an obsession with culinary craft. What started as a family BBQ spot has grown into one of Karachi's most beloved multi-cuisine destinations.

Our Head Chef, Ustad Rashid Khan, brings 25+ years of culinary expertise — mastering traditional Desi techniques while seamlessly blending continental finesse. Every dish is crafted with premium ingredients, sourced fresh daily.
